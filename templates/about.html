Our Story

Handcrafted with love, one stitch at a time

Wunlove.co was born from a simple love of creating. What started as a hobby of knitting tiny animals for friends quickly grew into a small business dedicated to bringing warmth and joy through handmade toys.

Every toy is carefully designed and knitted by hand using high-quality, soft yarn. We believe in slow craftsmanship - each piece takes hours of dedicated work to ensure it's perfect before it reaches your home.

What Makes Us Special

Handmade Quality

No factory production - each toy is uniquely made by hand.

Sustainable Materials

We use eco-friendly, non-toxic yarns that are safe for everyone.
